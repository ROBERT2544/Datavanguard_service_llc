Cookie Policy

Effective Date: June 23, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how DataVanguard Services LLC ("we", "our", or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website (https://datavanguardbi.com).

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help us recognize your device, enhance your experience, and gather usage data.

2. Types of cookies we use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality (e.g., session management, form submissions).
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site using analytics (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Functional Cookies: Remember choices and preferences (e.g., language, region).
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver personalized ads and track ad performance (e.g., Facebook Pixel, Google Ads).

3. How we use cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Keep our site secure and functional
  • Improve performance and loading speed
  • Analyze visitor behavior and usage patterns
  • Deliver relevant ads based on browsing history

4. Third-party cookies

Some cookies may be set by third-party services integrated into our site, such as:

  • Google Analytics
  • Facebook Ads
  • YouTube (embedded videos)

5. Your choices

You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings. Note that blocking cookies may impact your experience.

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Internet Explorer

6. Consent

By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy. If you do not agree, you should disable cookies or stop using the site.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date".
